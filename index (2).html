Research Interests

My research lies in graph theory and combinatorics. I work on extremal graph theory, including Turán-type problems, degree power sums, and spectral extremal problems for graphs forbidding paths, cycles, or matchings. I am also interested in the structural theory of tournaments and directed graphs, particularly arc-decompositions, path partitions, and long-standing conjectures such as the quasi-kernel conjecture and Seymour's second neighbourhood conjecture. Additional interests include graph coloring and orientation, combinatorial optimization, and Ramsey theory.
