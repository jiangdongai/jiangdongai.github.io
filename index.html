Research Interests

My research focuses on structural and combinatorial properties of directed graphs and hypergraphs. I am interested in colorings of digraphs, generalizations of undirected graph results to directed settings, and fundamental properties of directed hypergraphs including Hamiltonicity and king problems. I also work on spectral graph theory and extremal combinatorics.
